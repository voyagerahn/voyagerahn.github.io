Research

I'm interested in investigating how learning algorithms can enable robots to acquire complex skills and controlling how robots work these tasks well in real-world settings. My research goal is thus delve into learning and interpreting deep representations for complex skills and enabling machines to smoothly compose and execute skills for hierarchical tasks.
